About Uniforms

Jupiter Enterprise is one of the finest veterans of the uniform industry that is in the business since 1985. Throughout the years, Jupiter Enterprise has been known for its strategic thinking and efficient execution which in turn leads to a high level of quality and commitment. In the 35 years, the organization has built long-term relationships with several traders, retailers and individuals who shown immense trust in them.

Jupiter Enterprise ably lead the from by Mr. Vraj Shah, next generation enterpreneur who has been following his father's footsteps and has tirelessely worked to make the company one of the best in the business.

Our Vision

To manufacture products that match international standards, be customer-focused and a globally competitive brand through better quality, latest technology, and continuous innovation.

Our Mission

The company seeks a high standard of performance and aims to maintain a long term leadership position in the textile market segments in which it competes. This will be achieved through operating efficiency, continued dedication to customer care, cost-effectiveness, innovation and conformance to our values.
